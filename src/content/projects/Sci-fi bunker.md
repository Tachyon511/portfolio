## Overview
This environment was created based on a description of a bunker deep behind enemy lines in the dystopian sci-fi book series Altered Carbon. The bunker i split into 3 sections with a dedicated purpose and military functionality in mind, creating a believable environment.

Models were created and textured in Blender and the rendering was done in Unreal Engine 5.
